Welcome to the TRAA

The TRAA is a volunteer organization, governed by a formal constitution and by-laws. An elected Executive, with the guidenceof the General Membership, sets the policies and direction for the TRAA.The TRAA Works at a Grassroots Level Within the Thames River Watershed to:

Distribution

Protect, enhance and regenerate a viable, multispecies, year-round fishery.

Protection

Promote and participate in environmental clean-up, pollution control and rehabilitation.

Promotion

Promote environmentally sound, safety and respectful sport fishing practices in the Thames River.

Cooperation

Form partnerships with community businesses, government agencies,and other in the River.
